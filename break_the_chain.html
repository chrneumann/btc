Break the Chain is a web player for samba tunes of the Rhythms of Resistance network. It's still under development and may crash your browser, eat your cat, or start a revolution.

Changes

  • 2012/07/10: Published.

Problems?

Break the Chain will possibly not work for your browser or your operating system. In this case, you will hear no instruments at all or hear them very choppy / unrhythmic. This is due to the fact that Break the Chain uses modern techniques that are not yet well supported by most browsers. But it seems to work rather good on Google's web browser Chrome. Always use the latest update of your browser! Also try to restart your browser.

If you can't solve the problems you might try the Flash version of Break the Chain. It will work for most browsers, but the display of the notes will not be synchronized to the sound.

Supported browsers

  • Google Chrome 18,20 (on Linux, maybe also on other operating systems like Windows or MacOS)

Does it work for your browser which is not listed here? Contact me!
